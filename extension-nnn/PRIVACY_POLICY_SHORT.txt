PRIVACY POLICY - No Nut November Challenge Extension

Last updated: October 2024

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DATA COLLECTION

This extension does NOT collect any personal data.

All data is stored LOCALLY on your device using Chrome's storage API.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

WHAT IS STORED LOCALLY (ON YOUR DEVICE ONLY):

1. Challenge Progress
   - Days marked as success/failed
   - Streak count
   - Unlocked badges

2. User Preferences
   - Blocking on/off
   - Notifications on/off

3. Custom Blocked Sites
   - List of websites YOU chose to block
   - Remains private and local

4. Timestamps
   - Challenge start date

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT:

❌ NO personal information
❌ NO browsing history
❌ NO email addresses
❌ NO IP addresses
❌ NO analytics or telemetry
❌ NO cookies
❌ NO external data transmission

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DATA TRANSMISSION

The extension makes ZERO network requests to external servers.

Everything is processed locally by your browser.

No data leaves your device. Ever.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED

1. storage
   → Save your progress locally

2. declarativeNetRequest + declarativeNetRequestWithHostAccess
   → Block adult websites locally
   → Redirect to motivational page

3. notifications
   → Show optional daily reminders

4. alarms
   → Schedule notifications

5. <all_urls>
   → Required to block sites across all domains
   → Does NOT read page content
   → Does NOT collect browsing history

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

YOUR CONTROL

You have full control over your data:

• View: Open DevTools console → chrome.storage.local.get(null, console.log)
• Export: Settings → Export data (JSON)
• Delete: Settings → Delete all data OR uninstall extension

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SHARING CODE FEATURE

The "accountability" sharing code is generated LOCALLY based on your stats.

Format: NNN-2024-ABC12-15D12S
- ABC12 = Anonymous hash
- 15D = 15 days validated
- 12S = 12-day streak

NO personal data is included or transmitted.

None. Zero. Nada.

100% local and autonomous.
